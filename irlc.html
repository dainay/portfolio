Description

In 2019, I was offered to create the official brochure for the 65th anniversary of the Institute of Russian Language and Culture at Moscow State University. This brochure was intended to prosent the organization's history.

According to the director's vision, it had a square format, but the cover was designed to unfold into a historical timeline, illustrating all the significant events.

It was a challenging task, considering the strict and ever-changing preferences of the direction regarding colors, text, and composition. However, such conditions are typical for work closely related to clients so I see it as an enriching professional experience.
