Lion King

Role

Conception, Design, UI

Technologies

Photoshop, Figma

Type

Educational project

Year

2022 April

Description

The main goal of this assignment was to redesign a well-known website in such a way as to replace the graphical universe of the site with a completely opposite one. For this task, we initially recreated the layout of the current website in Figma, then analyzed its UI. Afterward, we were required to propose a mood board suggesting a design that would have no connections with the original version.

For this assignment, I chose the website of the musical "The Lion King," which I love. The universe of the old website is very strict, limited to three colors, and characterized by strict lines, which I replaced with color and shapeless patterns.
