As part of this project, we were tasked with creating an advertising campaign for one of the cities where one of the two students of the woorking group resides. To showcase the city, we needed to develop a poster, advertising banners, and a layout for a responsive website (desktop, tablet, and phone) based on a mood board. Also our misson was to create potentially interactive map of this plase which we could place on our site.

My teammate and I chose Melun for our project. Our concept revolved around portraying Melen not only as an administrative center but also as a cultural hub with a rich history and beautiful scenery.

For the posters, we selected the slogan "Rendew-vous avec ..." to highlight various aspects of the city. I decided to prepare five posters, each featuring one letter from the city's name, along with mockups. Additionally, I proposed a new logo for the city.

Following this, we prepared a layout for a potential website that reflected our advertising campaign.
