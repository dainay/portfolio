Description

Prix: 1 place "Best Educational Project"

During my work at the Institute of Russian Language and Culture, my two colleagues and I prepared an educational game for our foreign students. The game is designed for level A2 and invites students to take on the role of secret agents helping the world-famous chef Raul find the stolen recipe for his signature cake.

To immerse students in a more immersive environment, I created numerous visual materials for the game. Each student, for example, had a secret agent passport, a personal ID card, as well as various maps, crosswords, audio, and even a video message from our chef.

After the game, I compiled a video documenting how our experimental game unfolded. This project helped us win the competition for the best Educational Projects at the institute among Russian language teachers.
