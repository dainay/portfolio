UI/UX design

Back-end








During my education, I gained a rich experience with PHP language. Through numerous projects, we developed websites with database integration, displaying information on pages based on specified parameters. I also worked on APIs, robust user session management, and tailored diverse user experiences according to their roles on the site.

In organizing databases, I underwent training in constructing conceptual models. Based on the analysis, I created the corresponding physical data model what heleped me with deeper data base organisation

JavaScript

Frameworks

Graphic Design








I have rich experience in graphic design, both for educational purposes and personal and professional projects. During this time, I've worked on various formats, including brochures, magazines, posters, social media visuals, and more, catering to both web and print needs.

Photography








Photography became my primary hobby several years ago. Since then, I have dedicated a lot of time to self-study in the theory and post-processing, honing my skills through practical application. I have experience working both in studio setups with lighting and in outdoor environments.
