Description

This project propose students to create a magazine about their neighborhood or district. The task was to fully develop an authentic issue of a local newspaper focusing on the life of the place. My magazine presented the life in the Melun City Center. For the concept, I decided to create a special edition of a local newspaper that plans to describe each of the disctricts of Melun from a tourist perspective.

The majority of the project time was spent on writing the texts. One of the mandatory conditions for this project was to conduct interviews with one of the residents. I was fortunate to speak with two - the owner of a local store and also a worker from the tourist office. There weren't many quality photo materials available online with Melun, so I had to take on the role of a photographer eather.

The main layout was done in InDesign. I had to prepare two versions - gigital version and a documetn for the printing house. I also used InDesign to work with vector images and Photoshop for photo processing.

This project was quite challenging due to tight deadlines. However, it allowed me to delve into the details of print design and master the Adobe Suite at a higher level.
