2022 February-May

The urban project became the most extensive project of the first year of study at the MMI. This project involved a large number of different aspects that had to be implemented by a team of 4 people.

The main goal of the project was to find (or imagine) a charitable organization for which our team could propose a digital communication strategy. This strategy included a website created on the WordPress platform, posters and banners for printing and social media, video advertisement with elements of motion design, and, of course, a description of the methods, techniques, and platforms for potential communication with an analysis of the target audience.

Due to my deep concern for animal welfare, I proposed to my team that we assist a shelter. We selected the "Relais des Animaux" shelter, focusing primarily on redesigning their website.

I took on various roles throughout this project. For the WordPress website development, I and my teammate utilized the free version of the Elementor plugin, along with additional plugins that added functionalities like information collection from forms etc. The site was intended to function on the desktop version, as specified in the project details.

For representation the brand's visual identity, I created a brand book. I modernized the logo and produced several posters and banners to enhance communication.

The foundation of our video centered on my to narrate the real-life story of a dog that found itself in the shelter due to mistreatment by its owners. With my another teammate we edited the little video, blending actual footage with our animated character, Charlie.
