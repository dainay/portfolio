Description

As part of my Artistic Culture course, students in my year were tasked with designing a poster for the Festival "les Arts Forestiers 2023". Mandatory requirements included incorporating the event's logo, section titles, and logos of event partners. The theme of this year revolved around the interconnectedness of human roots and nature's trees.

I proposed two affiches. One of my posters was selected by organisators for official presentation at the festival.

Additionally, I was asked to prepare a poster for the second-year MMI graduation ceremony.
