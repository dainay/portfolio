As part of my Artistic Culture course, students in my year were tasked with designing a poster for the Festival des Arts Forestiers 2023. Mandatory requirements included incorporating the event's logo, section titles, and logos of event partners.

Out of all the submissions, my poster was selected for presentation at the festival.

Additionally, I was asked to prepare a poster for the second-year MMI graduation ceremony.
