Description

During the first semester, our primary project involved conducting an interview with a theatre professional. For our promotional theme of the semester, we chose "theatre." Our group of 6 students was excited to interview the Public Communication Manager of the Montreux Theater.

I took some photos during the set

Our task was not only to prepare questions and find a professional in the field but also to set up our shooting location, arrange lighting, prepare the camera and sound equipment. Afterward, each group member was responsible for presenting their individual editing.
