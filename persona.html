Description

As part of the university's Open House Day, we had the opportunity to practice UX and conduct interviews with incoming students to create a Persona profile. In a team of four, we interviewed 21 students, asking them personal questions, queastions about their gadget usage, social media habits, and general knowledge of digital culture.

After compiling and analyzing all the results, we managed to identify the user type of a potential student interested in the MMI program. This information could potentially be used for organizing future Open House events and updating the program's website.
